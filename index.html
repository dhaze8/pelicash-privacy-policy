Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 5 June 2025

Pelicash ("we", "us", "our", or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in connection with your use of the Pelicash mobile application.

Pelicash is designed to help you find and compare cashback offers and receive notifications about them. No account registration is required to use Pelicash.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, or physical address. To provide core functionalities and improve your experience, the app may collect or process certain information, including:

  • Device identifiers or tokens for delivering notifications and managing your preferences.
  • Information about your notification preferences and alert settings, which may be stored locally on your device or securely in the cloud.
  • Purchase or subscription status, if you choose to access premium features. Payment processing is handled by trusted third-party providers; we do not store your payment details.
  • Basic usage data, such as app features used or screens visited, to help us improve the app. This data is anonymized and does not personally identify you.
  • Advertising identifiers, if ads are displayed, to provide relevant advertising and measure performance. You can control ad personalization in your device settings.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain app functionality, including cashback comparison and notifications.
  • To deliver notifications and alerts you have configured (if you have granted permission).
  • To process in-app purchases or subscriptions (if applicable).
  • To display relevant advertising (if applicable).
  • To improve the app and user experience through aggregated, anonymized analytics.
  • To comply with legal obligations, if required.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited information with trusted service providers who help us operate the app, deliver notifications, process payments, or provide analytics and advertising. These providers are required to protect your information and use it only for the services they provide to us.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

5. Your Choices

  • You can manage your notification preferences within the app or through your device settings.
  • You can control personalized advertising through your device's privacy settings.
  • Subscriptions and purchases are managed through your app store account.

6. Children's Privacy

Pelicash is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that such information has been provided, we will take steps to delete it.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted within the app or on our website, and the effective date will be updated above. Please review this policy periodically.
